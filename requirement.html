Requirements for Candidacy

Enrollment Status

Must be a currently enrolled student of Southern Luzon State University Lucena Campus.

Academic Standing

Candidate must not have failing grades and must maintain good academic performance.

Good Moral Character

No record of major disciplinary actions or violations in the university.

Application Documents

Must submit a complete set of candidacy forms and requirements before the deadline.

Endorsements

Required to secure endorsement signatures from classmates or organizations.
