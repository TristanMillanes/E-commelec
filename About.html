About Campus e-COMELEC

Empowering Students Through Transparent and Digitalized Elections

Who We Are

The Campus e-COMELEC is the official Commission on Elections of the Southern Luzon State University Lucena Campus. We are dedicated to ensuring student government elections are conducted fairly, transparently, and securely with the use of digital platforms.

Our Mission

To provide a secure, transparent, and accessible voting system for students by integrating technology into the election process.

Our Vision

To become a model of digitalized student elections that promotes integrity, inclusivity, and democratic participation for all students.

Core Values

  • Integrity
  • Transparency
  • Inclusivity
  • Accountability
  • Service to Students
